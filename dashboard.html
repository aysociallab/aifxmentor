📖 Terminal Glossary

Understanding the institutional metrics used in AI Forex Mentor.

R-Multiple (R-Mult)

The ultimate measure of a trader's edge. It represents your profit relative to your initial risk. If you risk $100 on a trade and make $250, your R-Multiple is +2.5R. Prop firms prioritize traders who consistently achieve high positive R-Multiples over those with high win rates.

Realized P/L

Profit or Loss that has been officially locked in and permanently added to (or subtracted from) your Account Balance. This occurs when a specific Take Profit (TP1, TP2, TP3) or your Stop Loss is physically hit in the live market.

Live (Floating) P/L

The current unrealized profit or loss of active trades based on live market prices. This value fluctuates constantly and is only added to your Realized P/L when a target or stop is hit.

Estimated Risk (Est. Risk)

The exact dollar amount you will lose if your Stop Loss is hit. This is calculated dynamically using your Entry Price, Stop Loss, Asset Multiplier, Total Lot Size, and Broker Leverage.

Prop Firm Co-Pilot

A strict risk-management guardrail. When activated, the terminal will mathematically block you from logging any trade that violates your "Max Daily Drawdown %" limit, ensuring you never blow a funded challenge account.

AI Trade Autopsy

A post-trade analysis tool. When a trade concludes, click this button to have the AI review your entry and market context. It will provide a psychological and technical breakdown of why the trade likely succeeded or failed.
